Meet The Team

Our Current Team Of Developers Working On "Cyton"

Nathaniel Richards

Project Lead

I'm the project lead and lead programmer for the game "Cyton". I manage the games on track direction and I filter the core ideas that will be in the final product. I currently work in Unreal Engine, Photoshop, Autodesk Maya, and Substance Painter and Designer.





Nathanial Ruland

Audio Designer

I'm the audio designer for the game "Cyton" and I am in charge of creating the game's overall atmosphere through music and sound FX. I currently work with FL Studio 12 to create high quality sound assets and music for the game.





Jaime A. Aguirre

Lead Artist

I'm the lead artist and I make 3D props, characters, concept art, and much more to create horror environments that fit the game's core concept. Through art I bring to life the ideas of the team.
